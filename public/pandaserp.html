How It Works: High-Speed Pagination

We don't bypass the problem, we master it with superior speed.

  1. Initiate Search: A headless LightPanda instance is launched for the query.
  2. Scrape Page 1: The first 10 results are parsed instantly.
  3. Navigate & Scrape Loop: The API programmatically "clicks" next, loading and scraping pages 2-10 at unparalleled speed.
  4. Aggregate & Respond: All 100 results are combined into a single, clean JSON response.
